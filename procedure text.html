Jasmine Aromatheraphy Candles
(Biotechnology Product)


Materials: Tools:
  • Used Cooking Oil
  • Dried jasmine flowers
  • Stearin
  • Candle Wicks
  • Old crayons
  • Coconut Oil
  • Candle Molds
  • Cheesecloth
  • Thermometer
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Pot
  • Silicone Spatula
  • Bowl
  • Stove
Steps on how to make the essential oil:
  1. First, start by preparing the tools and materials.
  2. Next, the dried jasmine flowers are tied into a cheesecloth.
  3. The cheesecloth is soaked in a mixture of water and coconut oil.
  4. The cheesecloth is left in the mixture for 12 hours, and then the mixture is heated.
  5. The mixture is continuously boiled with the cheesecloth until the fragrance can be smelled.
  6. The cheesecloth is squeezed to collect the liquid when the fragrance can be smelled.
  7. The jasmine essential oil is prepared and ready for use in aromatic candles.
Steps on how to make the candle:
  1. All the tools and materials are prepared.
  2. The used cooking oil is heated over medium heat.
  3. Stearin is slowly added to the oil in a 1:2 ratio (oil to stearin) and stirred until dissolved.
  4. Old colored crayons are added to the pot and stirred until completely melted.
  5. Once everything is melted, the stove is turned off, and jasmine essential oil is added while stirring to combine.
  6. The mixture is allowed to cool slightly.
  7. The mixture is poured into silicone molds with wicks already placed.
  8. The candles are allowed to solidify completely.

Ruffled Batik Headbands


Materials: Tools:
  • Batik Fabric
  • Baking Paper
  • Sewing Thread
  • Plain Headband
  • Blas Tape
  • Scissors
  • Sewing needle
  • Pins
  • Fabric Pencil
  • Glue gun
  • Safety pin
  • Fabric scissors
  • Pencil
  • Ruler
Steps:
  1. All the tools and materials are prepared.
  2. A pattern is created on baking paper measuring 96 cm x 7 cm.
  3. The pattern is cut out and attached to the batik fabric using pins.
  4. The fabric is cut according to the pattern, then folded in half.
  5. The fabric is basted along the pattern and the pinned edges are sewn.
  6. The sewn fabric is turned inside out using a safety pin so that the stitches are hidden inside.
  7. The plastic headband is inserted into the fabric, and the fabric is pushed to create gathers.
  8. The fabric ends are glued with a hot glue gun and covered with bias tape.
